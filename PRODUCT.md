# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Design Skill Arena is for people browsing frontend design skill outputs: product builders, prompt authors, designers, and engineers who need to inspect how different skill chains shape the same product-page topic.

## Product Purpose

The product presents one fixed Muse campaign-workbench task across eighteen skill-chain combinations. Success means visitors can quickly understand each chain, open every generated result as an independent page, and inspect skill documentation without hidden backend state.

## Brand Personality

Precise, editorial, evaluative.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, fake dashboard screenshots, purple gradient AI styling, nested card-heavy layouts, decorative status dots, and UI that makes the collection look like a quality table.

## Design Principles

- Make the showcase structure visible before decoration.
- Treat every route and skill label as product data, not filler.
- Keep the arena visually quieter than the showcased Muse pages.
- Preserve independent result routes and new-tab result opening.
- Prefer dense, readable controls over marketing-style spectacle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, responsive layouts without horizontal overflow, reduced-motion support, and text contrast suitable for WCAG AA.
